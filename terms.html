Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Key Point: Creator Dashboard is a client-side application. We do not have a backend server. All your data stays in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

1. Information We Access

With your explicit permission, we access:

  • YouTube: Channel statistics (subscribers, views), video metadata, and performance metrics via YouTube Data API
  • TikTok: Profile statistics (followers, likes), video metrics, and account information via TikTok Open API

2. How We Store Data

Local Storage Only:

  • API credentials and access tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage
  • This data never leaves your device
  • You can clear this data anytime by clearing browser storage or clicking "Logout"
  • No data is stored on external servers or databases

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing activity
  • ❌ We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • ❌ We do not store your data on our servers (we don't have any)
  • ❌ We cannot access your accounts without your explicit authorization

4. Third-Party Services

We interact with official APIs from:

  • Google/YouTube: Subject to Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok: Subject to TikTok Privacy Policy

Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Since all data is local:

  • Your API tokens are as secure as your browser
  • We recommend using the Service on a personal, secure device
  • Do not share your computer while logged into the dashboard
  • Always revoke API access if you stop using the Service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we have about you (stored locally in your browser)
  • Delete all data by clearing localStorage or browser cache
  • Revoke API access through your Google and TikTok account settings
  • Export your data (visible in the dashboard interface)

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted in the dashboard.

For privacy concerns or data questions:

10. How to Revoke Access

To stop the Service from accessing your data:

  • YouTube: Visit Google Account Permissions and remove access
  • TikTok: Visit your TikTok settings → Security → Authorized Apps
  • Local Data: Clear browser storage or click settings → "Clear All Data"
